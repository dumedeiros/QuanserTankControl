Spark Charts

Spark charts are "data-intense, design-simple, word-sized graphics" charts for embedding in a context of words, numbers and images. Whereas the typical chart is designed to show as much data as possible and is set off from the flow of text, spark charts are intended to be succinct, memorable and located where they are discussed. Their use inline usually means that they are about the same height as the surrounding text. Also, spark charts can be intensively used in space-efficient executive dashboards to show a lot of KPIs in a single view.

FusionWidgets XT offer three types of spark charts:

  • Spark lines
  • Spark columns
  • Spark Win/Loss chart

Salient Features
 
  • Trend-lines and trend-zones can be configured in spark line & spark column.
  • Ability to draw Win, Loss & Draw in spark Win/Loss chart.
  • Support for pre-defined palettes and custom single color palettes.
  • Option to color open, close, high/low plot in different colors.
  • Ability to show/hide open, close, high/low value.
  • Ability to show/hide open, close, high/low anchors.
  • Ability to highlight period length by showing colored background blocks. Customizable period length.
  • In win/loss chart, support to color scoreless games with a different color.
